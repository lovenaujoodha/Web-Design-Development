Volunteer

    We rely on the continuous support of animal lovers to help us today so we can achieve what we started and be here for all the animals tomorrow.

    People choose to volunteer for a variety of reasons, for some it offers the chance to give something back to the community or make a difference to the people around them. For others it provides an opportunity to develop new skills or build on existing experience and knowledge.



    Moreover, you can be a volunteer at AORD to help organising charity event which are held monthly, participate in our activities, helps to find new homes for the dogs, in promoting responsible pet ownership, take the dog for walk and take care of them. This small efforts from an individual will make the dogs feel less lonely and be more cheerful and happy. However, to be able to volunteer you must be at least sixteen years old.

    By volunteering at AORD you can give suggestions and views on our past or forthcoming projects for a healthier environment for the dog population.
