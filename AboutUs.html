About Us - Who we are?

     



    Our company is about a shelter named AORD which stands for Adoption Of Rescue Dogs, has been operating since 2001 with the objectives to promote kindness and to prevent or suppress cruelty to animals, to ensure animals protection and conservation in the context of humane environment. AORD shelter is located at Lallmatie, as the villages nearby have a lot of stray dogs so it is easy for the team to step on the field and rescue those dogs.We rescue stray, abused and sick dogs who are living in a very bad condition such as suffering from skin disease, broken bone or necessary treatment they need, then afterwards the dogs are given for adoption and fostering according to the company policy system.we also offer the service of taking in dogs from clients who can no longer take care of their pets. We have an estimate budget of Rs100,000 each year.
