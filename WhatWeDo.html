What we do?

Just like you, we love dogs and want the very best for every one of them!

Rescuing

We take in and rehome dogs as helping vulnerable dogs is our number one priority. We also take in dogs from individuals, for all sorts of reasons: from changes in accommodation and finances, to relationship breakdowns and family crises. Our vet team works 6 days a week providing veterinary care and lifesaving surgeries.

Sterilisation

Our vet teams provide sterilisations, vaccinations and treatment for sick and injured animals. We subsidised low cost sterilisations to pets of those who come from financially disadvantaged households. NO animal will ever be refused sterilisation because of lack of funds.

Education

Our Education Strategy acknowledges that animal welfare education and learning for both children and adults will be essential to achieving our vision where every animal is treated with compassion and respect, making it socially unacceptable to mistreat and be cruel to any animal. Through our education services, we impact over 150,000 people each year and focus on promoting kindness and empathy towards animals.
