Veterinary Clinic


    AORD has its own veterinary clinic which treat all the rescued dogs and pets who are sick. It provides the services of a veterinarian and facilities for the medical treatment, examination, surgery, diagnosis, grooming as well as general health care.

    It is important to take your dogs for a yearly consultation at the veterinary to have an update about your dogs health.
